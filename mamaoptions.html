Where can I find more great manuscripts?

Some of my favorite sites include the University of Pennsylvania's Penn in Hand (http://dla.library.upenn.edu/dla/medren/index.html), the Walters Art Museum's ExLibris (http://manuscripts.thewalters.org/) and e-codices (http://www.e-codices.unifr.ch/en). I like these in particular because they are clear about what you can do with the manuscripts (download and reuse). Not all sites are as clear.
